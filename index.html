Projects

T2Y - Trailer to you

Trailer 2 You is a trailer booking app operating similarly to Uber, tailored specifically for trailers, and currently deployed on a small scale in Australia (under testing). As a backend developer, I contributed to building robust functionalities catering to diverse user roles. Customers can book trailers with options to update, reschedule, extend, or cancel bookings, track deliveries live, and receive notifications about booking statuses. Licensees can manage employees with custom access controls, oversee trailer statuses, rate users, and perform CRUD operations on trailers and upsell items. Licensee employees handle assigned tasks like trailer delivery and profile updates, while admins, as super-users, monitor transactions, process payments, and manage refunds. The application ensures accessibility across web, Android, and iOS platforms, delivering a seamless and efficient user experience.
